Powerful Features for Your Savings Group

Discover how Mangi Vikoba+ can transform the way your community savings group operates.

Core Features

Everything you need to manage your savings group effectively

Group Management

Easily manage your savings group with powerful tools designed specifically for community-based financial groups:

  • Create and manage multiple savings groups
  • Add members and assign roles (chairperson, treasurer, secretary)
  • Track meeting attendance and schedule future meetings
  • Send invitations to new members
  • Configure group settings and rules

Savings Tracking

Record and monitor all savings activities with complete transparency:

  • Record member contributions in real-time
  • Track individual member balances
  • Monitor overall group savings and growth
  • Set contribution targets and track progress
  • Generate savings statements for members

Loan Management

Streamline the entire loan process from application to repayment:

  • Process loan applications with customizable approval workflows
  • Calculate interest automatically based on group rules
  • Track loan repayments and outstanding balances
  • Set up repayment schedules and send reminders
  • Handle late payment penalties according to group policies

Financial Reports

Generate comprehensive reports to keep everyone informed:

  • Create detailed financial statements for the group
  • Generate individual member statements
  • Calculate and distribute profits fairly
  • Export reports as PDF for sharing or printing
  • Track group performance over time with visual charts

Additional Features

More tools to enhance your savings group experience

Smart Notifications

Never miss important events with automated reminders for meetings, payment due dates, and loan repayments.

Learning Center

Access a wealth of educational resources to improve financial literacy and group management skills.

Secure Access

Role-based access ensures members can only see and do what's appropriate for their position in the group.

Performance Analytics

Track group growth, member participation, and financial health with intuitive analytics dashboards.

Offline Functionality

Continue working even without internet connection, with data syncing when connectivity is restored.

Multi-language Support

Use Vikoba+ in your preferred language, with support for English and Swahili (more languages coming soon).
