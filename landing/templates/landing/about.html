Our Story

How Mangi Vikoba+ began and our journey to empower community savings groups

The Beginning

Mangi Vikoba+ was born out of a simple observation: community savings groups, while incredibly effective for financial inclusion, were still using paper-based systems that were prone to errors, fraud, and inefficiency.

Our founder, having grown up in a community where savings groups were a lifeline for many families, saw the potential for technology to transform these groups and increase their impact.

Our Mission

At Mangi Vikoba+, we're on a mission to empower community savings groups with digital tools that enhance transparency, accountability, and efficiency, while preserving the social fabric that makes these groups so valuable.

We believe that when savings groups thrive, communities prosper. By providing accessible technology, we aim to increase financial inclusion and help more people achieve economic stability.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every community savings group has access to digital tools that make financial management simple, secure, and transparent.

Our goal is to support 10,000 savings groups across East Africa by 2025, impacting over 250,000 individuals and their families, and mobilizing over $50 million in community savings.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Vikoba+

Community First

We believe in the power of community and design our solutions to strengthen, not replace, social connections.

Trust & Transparency

We're committed to building tools that foster trust through transparency and clear communication.

Accessibility

We design our platform to be accessible to users with varying levels of tech literacy and different devices.

Innovation

We continuously innovate to solve the real challenges faced by savings groups in their daily operations.

Education

We believe in the transformative power of financial education and prioritize learning resources.

Empowerment

We aim to empower individuals and groups to take control of their financial futures.
